# Grade 7A early-warning demo data

This is fictional demonstration data. It uses first names only.

Upload the three CSV files together through Veriq’s Upload Data page.

## Intended early pattern

- Tendai, Chipo and Nyasha develop repeated absences in the current period.
- Their behaviour incidents rise at the same time, while Tariro and Farai remain stable.
- The three learners’ Mathematics continuous-assessment results decline before the final exam.
- The class Mathematics average and attendance decline, but the evidence does not diagnose a cause.

## What Beacon should do

Beacon should identify a class-level early intervention opportunity, cite the verified attendance, behaviour and assessment changes, acknowledge uncertainty, and recommend a supportive check-in plus targeted academic support.

## About pass-rate prediction

The progressive assessments are appropriate inputs for a future pass-rate forecasting model. The current MVP detects early risk and trends; it does not yet claim to make a statistically validated final-exam pass-rate prediction.
